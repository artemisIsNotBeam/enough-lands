welcome to the guide of find the card count and mana value home

If your deck is on moxfield great! Then skip to step 3. Otherwise, start on 1

  2. go into moxfield and create a new deck, when prompted paste your dec
  3. note your card count (60, 100, 80 if u play yorion)
  4. scroll down to under sample hand and note down your total mana value(i call it mv)
  5. go back to home thru the link and enter the sats
